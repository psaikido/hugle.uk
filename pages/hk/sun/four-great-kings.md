<div class="poem">
the four great kings  
my brothers  
who spurned  
my adoration  
do they not know  
the Lord of Death  
cares nought  
for seniority?  
  
Yama crowned me  
and now  
in the fierce blast  
of freedom  
I wear  
my shaven head  
</div>

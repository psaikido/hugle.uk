<div class="poem">
a great surge of love  
overwhelmed me  
Gaia herself  
streaming with rain  
the mother of us all
 

'how can I help you?'  
I asked  


'heal my children!'  
she replied
 

I myself  
streaming with tears
 

our pain
</div>

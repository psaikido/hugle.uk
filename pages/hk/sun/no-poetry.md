<div class="poem">
today I have no poetry  
of my own  
just the sunlight  
on my feet  
as I cradle  
my aching bones  
my head swimming  
in pools of longing  
the Godess gently warms  
my toes  
and reminds me
that it is  
from the ground up  
that yesterday  
is released
</div>

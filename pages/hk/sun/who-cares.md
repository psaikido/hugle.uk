<div class="poem">
who cares?  
my friend asked  
as we strolled  
and talked  
of music  
of poetry  
who cares?  


are we not the ones  
whose job it is  
to care  
to craft?  


to have a response  
to make it public  
to express  
come what may  
even if  
no-one cares?
</div>

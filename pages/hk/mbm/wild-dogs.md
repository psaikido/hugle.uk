<div class="poem">
wild dogs barking  
sleepless belly rumbling  
very late or very early?  
three quarter moon  
hangs over Ladakh  
mind rolls like the night train  
it's freight of worry  
and loneliness  
how I wish I knew
 
the way
</div>

---
title: guesthouse
written: 2006
layout: mbm
permalink: /mbm/guesthouse.html
---

<div class="poem">
yet another guesthouse  
left behind  
it's ok
 
unwanted luggage  
not carried  
it's easy
 
betrayal  
one day older
 
my heart  
one day lighter
</div>

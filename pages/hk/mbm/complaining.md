---
title: when complaining stops
written: 2005
layout: mbm
permalink: /mbm/complaining.html
---

<div class="poem">
when complaining stops  
the world floods in  
and I'm swept along  
by the tweeting of birds  
 
the murmuring street
 
the patter of rain
</div>

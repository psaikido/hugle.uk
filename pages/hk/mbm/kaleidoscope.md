<div class="poem">
yesterday's endless kaleidoscope  
conjured life stories  
revolving  
mutating  


tomorrow's hesitant mirages  
magic narative momentum  
appearing  
disappearing


today's unfolding bewilderment  
karmic quanderies unreal  
inspiring  
expiring
</div>

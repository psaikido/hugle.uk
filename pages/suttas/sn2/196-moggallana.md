title: "SN 51.14 Moggallﾄ］a Sutta: Moggallﾄ］a"
---

A group of rowdy monks are messing about. The Buddha sends Moggallﾄ］a with the instruction to terrify them! He goes and causes the mansion they are living in to quake and tremble by touching it with his toe. They duly become terrified. The Buddha arrives and explains how Moggallﾄ］a developed such powers through following the training.

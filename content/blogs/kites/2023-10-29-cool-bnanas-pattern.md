- -5, fly right
- bottom turn fly left with a 360 in the centre
- bottom turn fly right with a 360 in the centre
- bottom turn inverted slide to left

- from bottom left, fly centre rollover landing
- launch to top
- +2, hover
- divestop, -5 to tip landing

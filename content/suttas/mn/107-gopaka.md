#### MN 107 Gaṇaka Moggallāna Sutta: The whole path

A discourse to Gaṇaka Moggallāna on a gradual training of a monk.

The steps laid out:

- Morality
- Sense control
- Moderation in eating
- Vigilance
- Mindfulness and consciousness
- Overcoming of the five hindrances
- Jhana

#### SN 3.8 Mallikaa Sutta: Mallikaa

King Pasenadi talks with his Queen Mallikaa. They agree that they both hold
themselves as most dear. The Buddha hears about it and comments:

"One finds none anywhere dearer than oneself. Likewise each person holds
themselves most dear. Hence one who love themselves should not harm others."

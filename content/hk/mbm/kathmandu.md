---
bannerTitle: "Mahabodhi Moonlight" 
title: Kathmandu
written: 2006
type: hk/mbm
---

rainy night in Kathmandu  
ghostly cycle rickshaw  
looms and swishes
 
rattling past
 
the whole street  
suddenly goes dark  
all but this shop
 
shining

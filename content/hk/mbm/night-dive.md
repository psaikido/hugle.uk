night dive  
scary jump nervous  
black waters threaten  
serious scuba  
going down
 
floating depths haunted  
bio-luminescence  
flitting fins thrill  
the ghost waters
   
our torches raised  
shining through bubbles  
the skittering surface  
comes closer
 
in just one breath  
boundaries breached  
a bewilderment of stars  
ascending infinity
 
cosmic expanse  
of wonderment  
looping above  
looming below

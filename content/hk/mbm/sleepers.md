clickety clock  
night freighted  
rolling stock  
sleepy weighted  
nodding  
lolling  
to Bangkok
 
clockety clack  
lost towns  
losing track  
slumber bye  
wheel squeal  
steel spin whistle  
coming back
 
clackety click  
carriage groans  
eyelids thick  
loco motives  
shadow schemes  
human cargo  
of dreams

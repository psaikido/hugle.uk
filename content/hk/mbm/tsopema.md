we walked around  
the holy lake  
suddenly  
a dying dog
 
it's wounds horrific  
the pain and pathos  
of its last breathing
 
alone in the afternoon  
I returned and gazed  
then something inside  
let go

 
what if I really stopped  
asking the world  
to make me happy?

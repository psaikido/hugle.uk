---
bannerTitle: "Sunshine & Shadow" 
title: treason
written: 2019
type: hk/sun
---

I gave you my very heart  
trusting in our joy  
but then you said I tricked you!  
that I was capable of treason!  
that was the real betrayal  
it was you who tricked me  
into becoming a lightening rod  
discharging  
your ancient pain

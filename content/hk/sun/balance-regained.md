<div class="poem">
a miracle of balance regained  
my mind is not these things  
but not something apart  
from this abandoned sock  
this broken glass  
this passing traffic  


a miracle of balance regained  
like water  
like crystal  
like space  


what was all that  
that dragged me down?  
</div>
